Tips for Energy and Water Conservation

  • Unplug appliances when not in use.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances.
  • Regularly maintain your appliances for optimal performance.
  • Use cold water for washing whenever possible.
  • Set your refrigerator to the right temperature (around 37°F).
